Cookie Policy

Last updated: 24 July 2026 · Applies to thaiholidaybudget.com

Cookies and similar technologies (like your browser's local storage) let a website remember things between visits. This site keeps them to a minimum and only turns on advertising cookies if you agree.

Strictly necessary (always on)

A small amount of local storage on your own device remembers your theme (light/dark), your cookie choice, and your saved budget estimate. This never leaves your device and isn't sent to a server. The estimator can't work sensibly without it, so it's always on.

Advertising

Ads are served by Google AdSense, which may set cookies (including the DoubleClick/DART cookie) to display and measure ads and to show ads based on your visits to this and other sites. If you are in the EEA or UK, Google's certified consent tool (CMP) asks for your choice about ad personalisation, and personalised ads are only shown with your consent; without it you may see non-personalised ads. You can review how Google uses cookies in advertising, reopen your choices via Cookie Settings, and opt out via Google Ads Settings.

Analytics

We do not currently run third-party analytics or tracking pixels. If that ever changes, we'll update this policy and ask for consent where the law requires it.

Third-party content delivery

The site loads open-source libraries and fonts from public content-delivery networks (Tailwind CSS and Chart.js, and Google Fonts). Those providers may set their own cookies or process your IP address as part of delivering those files, under their own policies, which we do not control. See our Privacy Policy for more on third parties.

Managing your choices

You're in control. Open Cookie Settings at any time to review or change your ad-personalisation choices and see exactly what is stored on your device. You can also clear cookies and local storage through your browser's settings, and most browsers let you block cookies entirely.
