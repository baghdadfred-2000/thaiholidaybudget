This Cookie Policy explains how thaiholidaybudget.com uses cookies and similar technologies. We keep this to the bare minimum needed to make the estimator work. We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics.

1. What are cookies and local storage?

Cookies are small text files a website can store in your browser. "Local storage" is a related browser technology that stores small amounts of data on your device. Both can be used to remember information between page loads. Our own features rely on local storage for strictly functional purposes; in addition, our advertising partner Google AdSense may set third-party cookies, as described in Section 3.

These are set by Google, not by us, and we do not have access to the information they collect.

  • Common examples include Google's advertising and frequency-capping cookies (such as those on the doubleclick.net and google.com domains).
  • If you do not consent to personalised advertising, Google may still serve non-personalised ads, which use cookies for frequency capping, aggregated reporting, and fraud/abuse prevention.

You can control or opt out of personalised advertising at myadcenter.google.com, learn how Google uses cookies in advertising at policies.google.com/technologies/ads, and manage vendor choices at aboutads.info/choices or, in the EU, youronlinechoices.eu.

4. What we do not use

See our Privacy Policy for more on third parties.

You are in control of both the functional storage and the advertising cookies:
