Accessibility Statement

Effective date: 21 June 2026 · Applies to thaiholidaybudget.com

Genext Information Systems is committed to making thaiholidaybudget.com usable by as many people as possible, including people who rely on assistive technology. Accessibility is an ongoing effort, and we welcome feedback that helps us improve.

1. Conformance target

We aim to align with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 at Level AA. While we cannot guarantee full conformance at all times, these guidelines shape how we build and maintain the Service. This statement reflects our current status and is reviewed periodically.

2. Measures we take

  • Responsive, mobile-first layout that adapts from small phones to large screens and supports browser zoom and text resizing.
  • Semantic HTML with labelled form controls, so screen readers can announce each field.
  • Keyboard operability for the step navigation, buttons, sliders, and links.
  • High-contrast theme with neon accents chosen to stand out against the dark background; key totals are presented as text, not images.
  • No flashing or strobing content that could trigger photosensitive reactions.
  • Plain-language guidance at each step, with figures shown in both your chosen currency and Thai baht.

3. Known limitations

We are transparent about areas we are still improving:

  • Range sliders are easiest with a pointer; we provide numeric fields and labels alongside them and continue to refine keyboard fine-control.
  • The dark neon theme is intentionally high-energy; a dedicated high-contrast or light mode is on our roadmap.
  • Some emoji icons are decorative and may be announced by screen readers; we are improving their labelling.

4. Tips for a better experience

  • Use your browser's zoom (Ctrl/Cmd and +) to enlarge text; the layout reflows cleanly.
  • Every slider has a matching number you can read, and most selections can be overridden with direct numeric entry.
  • Your progress is saved on your device, so you can take breaks without losing work.

5. Feedback and contact

If you encounter an accessibility barrier, we would like to know so we can address it. Please contact support@thaiholidaybudget.com, describing the page, what you were trying to do, and the assistive technology you use. We aim to respond within a reasonable timeframe.

6. Updates

We review this statement periodically and update the effective date above when we make accessibility improvements.
