Commitment

Accessibility

Last updated: 24 July 2026 · Applies to thaiholidaybudget.com

We want everyone to be able to use Uncle Pong's Thailand Holiday Estimator — whoever you are and however you browse. We aim to meet the spirit of the WCAG 2.1 AA guidelines and keep improving.

What we've built in

  • Keyboard navigation throughout, with visible focus outlines.
  • Semantic headings and landmarks, plus labelled form controls so screen readers can announce each field.
  • Colour contrast aimed at WCAG AA for body text, and a light/dark theme you control with a one-tap toggle.
  • Meaning never carried by colour alone — icons and text labels back it up, and key totals are shown as text, in both your chosen currency and Thai baht.
  • Large touch targets and a responsive, mobile-first layout that reflows cleanly down to small phones and supports browser zoom and text resizing.
  • Respect for your prefers-reduced-motion setting, so animations ease off if you've asked your device to reduce motion, and no flashing or strobing content.
  • Descriptive alternative text on meaningful images.

Third-party content

Some content we don't fully control — most notably the advertising served by Google AdSense — may not always meet the same accessibility standards. You can review or change your ad-personalisation choices at any time in Cookie Settings.

Still not right for you?

Accessibility is never "done." If anything here is hard to use with assistive technology, please tell us and we'll fix it — use our Contact page or email support@thaiholidaybudget.com, and let us know the page, what you were trying to do, and the assistive technology you use.
