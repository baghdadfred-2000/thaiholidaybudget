This Privacy Policy explains how Uncle Pong's Thailand Holiday Estimator ("the Service", "we", "us", or "our"), available at thaiholidaybudget.com, handles information when you use it. The Service is operated by Genext Information Systems. We have built the Service to be private by design: it is a free, browser-based budgeting calculator that runs on your device, and we do not operate a back-end database that collects your inputs.

1. Summary

  • We do not require an account, and we do not ask for your name, email, or payment details.
  • The figures you enter are stored only in your own browser (local storage) so your estimate is preserved between visits. They are not transmitted to us.
  • We do not sell or rent personal data. The Service is free and supported by Google AdSense advertising, which is provided by Google and may use cookies and similar technologies (see Section 4).
  • A small number of open-source assets are loaded from third-party content-delivery networks to make the page work.

2. Information we process

2.1 Information you enter

The estimator lets you enter details such as a nickname, number of travellers, destination, dates, currency, and various budget figures. This information is saved in your browser's local storage on your device. It stays there until you clear it. We do not receive or store it on any server we control.

2.2 Information collected automatically

We do not run our own analytics. However, our advertising partner Google (see Section 4) and any third-party CDN process technical data — such as your IP address, device and browser type, and the time of the request — as a normal part of serving ads and delivering web content. Google may also use cookies or device identifiers to measure and personalise advertising. This processing is governed by Google's own privacy policy, which we do not control.

2.3 Information we do not collect

We do not knowingly collect contact details, financial account information, location data, or any special categories of personal data.

3. How we use information

Because the information you enter never leaves your device, we do not use it for any purpose. Its sole function is to power the on-screen calculation and to remember your progress for your convenience. We do not profile, target, or contact you.

When these assets and ads are requested, the providers may process your IP address and request metadata under their own privacy policies, which we do not control. You can review how Google uses information from sites that use its services at policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites. You can manage or opt out of personalised Google advertising at myadcenter.google.com, and opt out of personalised ads from many vendors at aboutads.info/choices and youronlinechoices.eu (EU).

6. Data retention

Because your data is stored locally, you control how long it is kept. It remains in your browser until you use the in-app "Reset" button, clear your browser's site data, or use a private/incognito window. We do not hold copies.

Because we do not hold your personal data on any server, you can exercise these rights directly and immediately: view, edit, export (PDF), or delete your information from within the Service itself. The Service is intended for adults aged 18 and over and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe a child has provided information through any contact channel, please contact us so we can address it.
